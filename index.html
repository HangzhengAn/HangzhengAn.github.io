Undergraduate academic research

Analysis of the Distribution Relationship between Urban Fitness Space and Infrastructure Based on XgBoosts and SHAP

Taking Shanghai as an example, after crawling POI (Point of Interest) data using a crawler, the data is imported into ArcGis for kernel density analysis, grid processing, city boundary clipping, projection transformation, and coordinate correction. After training the model using XGBoost, SHAP (Shapley Additive exPlanning) is used to explain the model.
